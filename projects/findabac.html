Find-A-Bac

[ A Tool For Digital Pathology And Image Analysis ]

Allowing pathologists to scan slides for mycobacterium automatically and get results in the fraction of the time taken to complete a manual scan. Using Convoluted Neural Networks for computer vision


What does it offer:

Find-A-Bac offers Automatic Mycobacterium Detection, supported by Batch File Uploading and Extensive File Format Support Including .tiff and .isyntax. Find-A-Bac has Multi-GPU Support, Allowing your system to excel at scanning multiple slides at once. It also comes with a simplistic GUI for easy use and learning, while also allowing deeper analysis of the results using the Task Viewer, to view a heatmap of the detection locations along with the individual detection locations in full resolution. Find-A-Bac has two versions; Find-A-Bac Local for individual / small groups and Find-A-Bac Business for companies that need high throughput scanning and analysis.



Find-A-Bac Local

Find-A-Bac Minimal comes with the latest neural network model, allowing for faster scanning and analysis. Its web interface is simple and easy to use, allowing for quick scanning and analysis. Find-A-Bac Minimal also comes with a Task Viewer, that displays a colour map of where has been scanned by the tissue selection algorithm - Although it does not support heatmap generation.
